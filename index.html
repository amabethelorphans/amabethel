About Us

At Ama Bethel Orphans Care Initiative, we are committed to providing comprehensive care, quality education, and spiritual nurturing to orphaned and vulnerable children in Arhan Kunu, Mubi, Adamawa State.

Founded in 2021 by Rebecca John Mandafia, our initiative was born from a deep compassion for children who lacked basic welfare and educational opportunities. We believe every child deserves a chance to thrive, regardless of their circumstances.

We are a faith-based, community-rooted organisation that partners with local families, churches, and international supporters to ensure no child is left behind. Our holistic approach addresses not just immediate physical needs, but emotional healing, educational advancement, and spiritual growth.
